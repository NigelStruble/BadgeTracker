# Badge of Justice Tracker

A World of Warcraft TBC Classic addon that helps you track Badge of Justice boss kills and loot collection.

## Features

- **Automatic Boss Detection**: Tracks when you kill any heroic dungeon boss that drops a Badge of Justice
- **Loot Reminders**: Displays a visual and audio alert when you kill a boss, reminding you to loot your badge
- **Daily Tracking**: Shows which bosses you've killed and looted from today (resets with heroic dungeon lockouts)
- **Minimap Icon**: Convenient minimap button for quick access
- **Comprehensive Database**: Includes all heroic dungeon bosses that drop badges across all TBC dungeons
- **Visual Interface**: Clean UI showing your progress organized by dungeon

## Installation

1. Download or extract the `BadgeTracker` folder
2. Place the folder in your WoW addons directory:
   - Windows: `C:\Program Files (x86)\World of Warcraft\_classic_\Interface\AddOns\`
   - Mac: `/Applications/World of Warcraft/_classic_/Interface/AddOns/`
3. Restart World of Warcraft or reload your UI with `/reload`

## Usage

### Minimap Icon
Look for the Badge of Justice icon on your minimap!
- **Left-click**: Open/close the tracker window
- **Right-click**: Reset daily progress (with confirmation)
- **Hover**: See today's kill/loot summary
- **Drag**: Reposition the icon around the minimap

### Opening the Tracker
You can also type `/badge` or `/badgetracker` in chat to open the tracker window.

### Understanding the Display

The tracker shows all heroic dungeons and their bosses with status indicators:

- **Gray text**: Boss not yet killed today
- **Orange text with [!]**: Boss killed but badge not yet looted
- **Green text with [X]**: Boss killed and badge looted

### Features

- **Daily Reset**: The tracker automatically resets when heroic dungeon lockouts reset (works for all regions)
- **Manual Reset**: Click the "Reset Daily" button in the tracker or right-click the minimap icon (with confirmation dialog)
- **Real-time Updates**: The tracker updates automatically as you kill bosses and loot badges
- **Summary**: See your total kills and loots for the day at the top of the window
- **Minimap Icon**: Toggle the minimap icon visibility using the checkbox at the top-right of the tracker window

### Alerts

When you kill a boss that drops a Badge of Justice:
- A raid warning appears in the middle of your screen
- A chat message is posted
- A sound effect plays to grab your attention

## Dungeons Tracked

The addon tracks all heroic dungeon bosses from:
- Hellfire Citadel (Ramparts, Blood Furnace, Shattered Halls)
- Coilfang Reservoir (Slave Pens, Underbog, Steamvault)
- Auchindoun (Mana-Tombs, Crypts, Sethekk Halls, Shadow Labyrinth)
- Tempest Keep (Mechanar, Botanica, Arcatraz)
- Caverns of Time (Old Hillsbrad, Black Morass)
- Magisters' Terrace

## Tips

- Keep the tracker window open during heroic runs to see your progress in real-time
- The addon helps ensure you don't miss any badge loots during fast-paced runs
- Use this to plan which dungeons you still need to run for the day

## Troubleshooting

If the addon isn't working:
1. Make sure the addon is enabled in the character select screen
2. Type `/reload` to reload your UI
3. Check that you're in a heroic dungeon (badges only drop from heroic mode)
